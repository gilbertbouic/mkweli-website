FAQ

Straight answers for compliance teams

Is Mkweli a full AML compliance system?

No. It is a focused sanctions name-screening tool. You still need policies, risk assessment, transaction monitoring, SAR processes, and governance appropriate to your licence and regulator.

Does it require internet?

Screening works offline with embedded lists. Internet is only needed to download the app and to refresh lists from official sources.

Which lists are included?

United States OFAC SDN, United Nations Security Council consolidated list, European Union financial sanctions, and the United Kingdom FCDO sanctions list.

Is the app really free?

Yes. The Android APK is free to download and use. There is no per-seat licence fee.

Can we use it alongside our existing vendor?

Yes. Many teams use Mkweli as a mobile / offline complement to core systems — for branches, agents, or contingency screening.
